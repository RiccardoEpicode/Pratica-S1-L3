Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet carmamelized onions, tart Cranberries, and creamy Brie cheese. It will become your favourite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberries Recipies That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup of extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the Topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a pairing knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1/2 cups of fresh or frozen Cranberries

Method

  1. Make the Focaccia Dough

    2. In a medium sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients untill the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
